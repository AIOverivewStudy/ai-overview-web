 Best Vehicles for Commuting to Work 

 
 

 










No matter if your commute to work takes you down the road or across the entire county – choosing the right vehicle for the drive is essential to your enjoyment behind the wheel. After all, there’s no denying the fact that most adults spend a solid chunk of their weekdays, work weeks, and months throughout the year simply driving to and from work. 


Since you spend so much of your valuable time commuting to work, it’s important to choose the right type of vehicle for the task. This doesn’t mean having to settle for a bare-bones, zero-option, basic mode of transportation! Our team at the Germain Motor Company has created this helpful resource guide that lists our top picks for the best commuter cars currently available on the market. From comfortable and connected to efficient and exhilarating, you’ll have no trouble finding a great vehicle that’s perfect for your commute to work!

Best Hybrid Commuter Car – Toyota Prius


Arguably the most famous and well-known hybrid model of all time, the legendary Toyota Prius combines efficiency with versatility to help you make the most of your daily drive to work. It all starts with its revolutionary hybrid powertrain, boasting up to a 57 combined MPG fuel economy rating. Ready to go the distance, choosing the Prius means you won’t have to spend nearly as much of your hard-earned money at the gas station. In addition to its jaw-dropping efficiency, the Prius stands out with its clever rear liftback design that’s perfect for accommodating all of your daily essentials or if you need extra space when carpooling with your coworkers. Best of all, you’ll be able to drive with complete confidence from knowing that you’re never alone on the road thanks to standard Toyota Safety Sense driver-assist technologies. You’ll be able to commute to and from work with total peace of mind when you choose the Toyota Prius.

Best Compact SUV for Commuting – Honda CR-V


Sometimes, a little bit of extra space can make a world of difference, especially when you’re faced with a lengthy or stressful commute to your job. This is precisely the reason why we’ve chosen the popular Honda CR-V as our top choice for the best compact SUV for commuting. One of the main advantages that comes from commuting with a compact SUV is the expanded interior dimensions that result in greater headroom, greater legroom, more cargo space, and a raised driving position for superior visibility. Under the hood lies its standard 1.5-liter turbocharged 4-cylinder engine that can also be paired with the confidence of a proven all-wheel drive system, ensuring that even Mother Nature won’t prevent you from making it to work safely and on time. 


 New CR-V Inventory


















Best EV for Commuting – Volkswagen ID.4


Have you ever dreamed of never having to spend another dime on gasoline again? How about the convenience of waking up to a vehicle that’s ready to go the distance every morning without the need to stop and fill up and risk being late to your meeting? When you drive a Volkswagen ID.4, these dreams can become reality! Its sophisticated all-electric powertrain produces zero emissions along with a total range rating of up to 291 miles, meaning you’ll never have to worry about range anxiety. Take advantage of its cutting-edge charging solutions, making it simple to charge at home in your garage or driveway or simply recharge at your office once you arrive at work. Different from most EVs that feature polarizing looks, the dynamic Volkswagen ID.4 features a futuristic design that includes flowing character lines and even an illuminated front badge to announce your arrival.


 New ID.4 Inventory


















Best Luxury Sedan for Commuting – Volvo S60


It’s important to remember that, especially if you have a lengthy commute to work, we spend more time than you might think behind the wheel of our vehicles on a daily basis. Considering this fact, it might be worthwhile to enjoy some premium comfort amenities and revolutionary connectivity features found on a luxury sedan like the Volvo S60. Our top choice for this particular segment, the S60 stands out with a remarkably efficient turbocharged engine. Other upscale amenities offered on the S60 that are sure to be appreciated by commuters include a Bowers & Wilkins premium audio system, in-cabin air purification, Google built-in infotainment software, and a comprehensive suite of active safety features designed to keep you one step ahead of danger on the highways. Spacious seating for up to 5 adults makes the Volvo S60 the ultimate choice for your daily drive.
