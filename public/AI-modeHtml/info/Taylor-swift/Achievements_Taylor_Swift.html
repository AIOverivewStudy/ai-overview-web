Just a few accomplishments...

  • At age 10 she had her first performance on stage.
  • At age 13 Taylor signed her first music deal with a major label
  • At age 17:
  1. Taylor releases her single "Tim McGraw"
  2. Debut Album "Taylor Swift" is released
  • At age 18:
  1. Taylor wins Artist/Songwriter of the year at Nashville Songwriters Association
  2. Taylor wins the CMA Horizon Award
  3. Taylor achieves her first NUMBER 1 single through "Our Song"
  • At age 19:
  1. Taylor receives a Best New Artists nomination at the 2008 Grammy Awards
  2. Taylor appears on the list of Maxim Hot 100
  3. Taylor wins the Academy of Country Music Award for Top New Female Vocalist
*Taylor Swift has won 20 Teen Choice Awards out of 37 nominations since 2008*
  • At age 20:
  1. Her concert ticket to Los Angeles "Fearless" sells out in 2 minutes!
  2. Her song "Love Story" became the most paid download song in history.
  3. "Love Story" became first country song to hit NUMBER 1 in Mainstream Top 40 Chart.
  4. "Love Story" won the video of the year at the CMA
  5. Taylor makes history by becoming the youngest person to ever win the "Album Of The Year" at the Academy of Country Music Awards.
  6. Her album "Taylor Swift" becomes the 2nd best selling country album in history.
  7. Her album "Fearless" became the best selling album in US in 2009.
  8. "You Belong With Me" wins the MTV Video Music Awards for Best Female Video
  9. Taylor is the NUMBER 1 in Forbes Top Selling Musicians of 2008.
  • At age 21:
  1. Her 3rd album "Speak Now" sells over 1 Million copies in its first week of release.
  2. The "Fearless" album helped Taylor win 4 Grammy Awards
  3. "Fearless" won the Album Of The Year at the Grammy Awards
  4. "Fearless" also won the Grammy Award for Best Country Album
  5. "White Horse" help her win the Best Female Country Vocal Performance and Best Country Song at the Grammy Awards
  • At age 23:
  1. Her 4th album "Red" sells more than 1.2 million copies in the first week of release.
  2. "Mean" wins the Best Country Solo Performance and Best Country Song at the Grammy Awards
  3. Her album Speak Now becomes the fastest selling digital album by a female artist
  4. Taylor is NUMBER 1 in Forbes Highest Paid Celebrities Under 30
  5. Wins MTV Europe Music Award for Best Look
  6. Wins MTV Europe Music Award for Best Female
  • At age 24:
  1. Taylor's "Red Tour" won the best tour award at the Billboard Mid-Year Awards
  2. Her album "Red" wins the Favorite Billboard 200 NUMBER 1 Album at the Billboard Mid-Year Awards
  3. "Safe and Sound" wins the Best Song Written for Visual Media at the Grammy Awards
  4. "I Knew You Were Trouble" wins the Best Female Video at the MTV Video Music Awards.
  5. Wins Billboard Music Award for Top Female Artist
*Taylor Swift is only 24 years old and has accepted 227 awards*
  • At age 25:
  1. Her song "We Are Never Ever Getting Back Together" becomes the fastest selling single in digital history
  2. Taylor Swift becomes the first female solo artist to have 2 albums sell more than 1 million copies in the first week.
  3. Taylor is NUMBER 1 on Billboard's Music's Top 40 Money Makers 2014
  4. She is also NUMBER 1on People's Best Dressed
