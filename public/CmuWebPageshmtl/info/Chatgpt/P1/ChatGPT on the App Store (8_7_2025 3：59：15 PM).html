Amazing for college

I had taken a break from school for a few years and I was nervous to get back and finish my degree because the few classes I had left were graduate level classes and an upper level statistics class (never taken it before in my life, plus I couldn’t afford to take the beginning stats class). I decided to just start off by taking one class to start (the hardest one) which was statistics. If I didn’t have this app I would have failed a long time ago. I have an A+ in the class and the semester ends in one month. I asked this app to quite literally take every concept that was thrown at us and “dumb it down for me” and it made learning all those concepts so much easier! I can even put in the text from the class PowerPoint and upload my homework relating to that PowerPoint (because if you just ask it mathematical statistics questions some times it answers them to advanced then where we were at in that point in time) and it would break the problem down step by step for me with explanations. It would give me practice problems that look like my homework so I could prepare for the tests. Not only does it help with school, but I have asked it a million other random questions that I normally would have typed into safari and then had to read a bunch of different articles to get my answer (and even then I could still have read incorrect information). But this just spits out amazing answers to questions with very helpful explanations when needed. 10/10
